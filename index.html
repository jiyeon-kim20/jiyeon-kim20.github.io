Jiyeon Kim

Ph.D. student

Department of Geography, University at Buffalo

My name is Jiyeon Kim. I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Geography at University at Buffalo (UB). I am conducting research in the GeoAI lab under the supervision of Dr. Yingjie Hu. My research interests include GeoAI, disaster resilience, and wildfires.
